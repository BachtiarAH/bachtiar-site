# Hello World! 🚀

Welcome to my new blog! I'm Bachtiar Arya Habibie, a full-time Software Engineer based in Madiun. I recently decided to revamp my personal portfolio and blog, and what better way to do it than by exploring the speed and developer experience of **Astro** paired with **Tailwind 4**. 

## Why Astro?

As someone who works across different stacks—from React and Laravel to managing my own VPS infrastructure—I wanted a framework that delivers pure HTML with minimal JavaScript overhead by default, while still allowing me to sprinkle in interactive components when needed. Astro fit perfectly for this digital garden. 

## What to Expect Here

This space will serve as my personal notebook and a public log of the things I'm currently tinkering with. A few topics you can expect me to cover:

* **Web Development:** Deep dives into frontend architecture, Tailwind 4 styling, and building highly functional applications.
* **Local AI & Infrastructure:** My experiments with running local AI models (like Gemma 4 and TurboQuant) on standard hardware, setting up headless mini PCs, and taming VPS environments.
* **Personal Projects:** Updates on my ongoing work, including the Aksara Jawa education app I've been building and various automated scheduling systems.
* **Off-Screen Life:** Occasionally, I might share some thoughts on double-entry bookkeeping for personal finance, deep mechanics analysis for games like Dota 2 (always analyzing those high-skill-cap heroes), or just share a picture of my cat lounging in a sweater.

Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to explore the site, and stay tuned for more technical write-ups and project logs.
